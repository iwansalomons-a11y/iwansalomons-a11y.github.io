About us

Amsterdam's first student hockey club

ASHC Ad Fundum was founded in 2026 as Amsterdam's first official student hockey club. We are an informal association within AMVJ, one of the oldest hockey clubs in the city.

Whether you're an experienced player or have never held a stick before — you are welcome here. We combine serious training with the typical Amsterdam student atmosphere.

Together we are building something new. Be part of it from the very start.

The name
Ad Fundum

Latin — "to the bottom"

Ad Fundum is a Latin expression that literally means "to the bottom" — and in Dutch student culture it refers to emptying your glass in one go. It's an ode to student life: giving everything you've got, having fun together, and always going all the way.

That is exactly the spirit with which ASHC was founded. Not half-heartedly, but fully. On the pitch and beyond.

Ad Fundum is more than just a name. It's a mentality.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I join with my team? ▾
Yes, absolutely! You can join with an existing team, but also individually. We help with forming teams.

👥 Tip: Teams of 16+ pay no registration fee.
When do you start? ▾
We are starting this season with training, matches and events. Exact dates coming soon — join the waitlist to be the first to know.
What if I don't know anyone? ▾
No problem. Most people join on their own. That's exactly why this is the perfect place to meet new people.
How do I sign up? ▾
Sign up via the waitlist at the top of the page. The earlier you register, the greater your chance of a spot in the first cohort.
How serious is it / what levels are there? ▾
We aim to form teams so that everyone plays at their own level. Competitive teams can also train twice a week.
What does it cost? ▾
Match membership: €350 per season
Training membership: €225 per season
Non-hockey membership: €75 per season

Registration fee: €25 (one-time — waived for teams of 16+)

🎁 April offer: Free club shirt when you sign up in April.
Do you also organise parties and socials? ▾
Definitely. Besides hockey we organise drinks, parties and socials. This is an important part of the club.

We are also looking for committee members. Interested? Email us at info@ashcadfundum.nl.
How accessible is the location? ▾
AMVJ is centrally located in Amsterdam and easily reachable by bike. The club is closest from Museumplein and next to Uilenstede.

You can also park by car. Bonus: shared scooters can be parked at the entrance of AMVJ.
Is this AMVJ or ASHC? ▾
ASHC is a student association within AMVJ. So you play on AMVJ's pitches, but you are part of ASHC.

This makes us unique: the first and only student hockey club in Amsterdam.
Why ASHC? ▾
There is currently no student hockey club in Amsterdam. Many students play hockey scattered across the city — that could be more fun and better organised.

We are building a club where you play, train and organise events together with other students. Hockey and social life come together here.
Can I get involved in the club? ▾
Yes. We work with various committees, such as:

- Events committee
- Bar committee
- Hockey & events
- Referee committee
- Membership administration

Sign up via the waitlist or email info@ashcadfundum.nl.
Can I join for half a season? ▾
Yes, for example if you are travelling or studying abroad for a semester.

Check below which membership types are available.
What membership types are there? ▾
We offer different membership types:

Match membership — €350
Participate in all training sessions and matches.

Training membership — €225
Join training every Thursday and all events. May play up to 3 matches per season.

Semi-membership
Fully participate for half a season. Ideal if you're going travelling.

Non-hockey membership — €75
Be part of the club without playing hockey. Join drinks and events, no matches.
Is sponsorship possible? ▾
Yes, we are open to collaborations and sponsorship. Get in touch via info@ashcadfundum.nl.
